# Product Vision

## What is Muse?

Muse is a dedicated smart wardrobe that transforms the way people organize, visualize, and plan their outfits.

Designed to run on a Raspberry Pi powered touchscreen device, Muse provides a beautiful digital representation of a user's wardrobe, allowing them to browse clothing, create outfit combinations, and prepare what they will wear without physically searching through their wardrobe every day.

Muse is designed as a standalone household product rather than just another mobile application.

---

## Who is Muse for?

Muse is designed for anyone who wants a simpler and more organized way to manage their wardrobe.

It is especially valuable for:

- People with busy schedules who want to prepare outfits in advance.
- People who enjoy planning and staying organized.
- Fashion enthusiasts who like experimenting with different outfit combinations.
- People with mobility or accessibility needs who benefit from reducing unnecessary physical effort.
- Families looking for a visual way to organize clothing.

---

## Why should Muse exist?

Choosing what to wear is a task repeated thousands of times throughout life.

Despite this, wardrobes have remained almost entirely physical and disconnected from modern digital tools.

Muse exists to bridge that gap by giving people a simple, visual, and enjoyable way to organize their clothes, prepare outfits, and save time every day.

Instead of replacing the wardrobe, Muse enhances it.

---

## Vision Statement

Muse brings the wardrobe into the digital age.

It is not an app that people occasionally open on their phone.

It is a dedicated product that is always ready when they stand in front of their wardrobe.
